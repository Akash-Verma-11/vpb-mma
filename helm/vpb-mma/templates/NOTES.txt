VPB-MMA has been deployed to namespace {{ .Values.namespace }}.

1. Check pod status:
     kubectl get pods -n {{ .Values.namespace }}

2. Get the ALB hostname (can take 2-3 minutes to appear):
     kubectl get ingress vpb-mma-ingress -n {{ .Values.namespace }}

3. Point your domain at that ALB hostname with a Route53 Alias A record
   (see the Chapter 4 guide, Step 6).

4. Once DNS resolves, visit:
     https://{{ .Values.domainName }}
